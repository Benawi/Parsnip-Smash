## Turnip Smash Mod for Minecraft 1.11.2
---
This simple mod adds the turnips Peach can pull out of the ground to Minecraft.
Depending on the face of the turnip they all deal different amounts of damage and knockback.
Additionally, there is a chance to pull out a bomb.
